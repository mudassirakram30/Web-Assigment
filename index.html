Introduction to Forex:

The foreign exchange market (Forex or FX) is the largest and most liquid financial market in the world, where currencies are bought and sold 24 hours a day. It plays a vital role in global trade and investment by determining currency exchange rates. In Forex trading, participants aim to profit from changes in currency prices — for example, buying one currency while selling another. Unlike the stock market, Forex has no central exchange and operates globally through a network of banks, brokers, and traders. With proper knowledge and strategy, Forex offers great opportunities for individuals to earn by understanding market trends and price movements.
